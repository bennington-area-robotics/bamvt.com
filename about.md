## About Bennington Area Makers

**Vision:** BAM aims to be an accessible, open, and safe space for the community to explore science, technology, engineering, arts and mathematics (STEAM) skills.

**Mission:** We provide opportunities for connection to the entire community via intellectually and physically creative activities.

## Founding & Background

Established formally in fall 2019, BAM emerged from grassroots community initiatives. The organizing committee — comprising James Salerno, Jeannie Jenkins, Ray Coulter, Kimberly Griffin, and Chris Callahan — formalized the organization with support from the Bennington County Regional Commission.

## Community Partners

BAM collaborates with local institutions including:

- Town of Bennington
- BCRC/IC (Bennington County Regional Commission)
- Community College of Vermont
- Southern Vermont State University
- The Lightning Jar
- StartUp 802

## Our Role

BAM addresses community needs for intergenerational connection and entrepreneurial development. We bring together people of all ages and backgrounds to learn, create, and build — strengthening our community through hands-on making.
